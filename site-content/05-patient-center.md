**URL:** https://bilskidental.com/patient-center
**Tagline:** Exceptional – Personalized – Refreshing

## Philosophy
Patient satisfaction is the top priority. Every patient receives care tailored to their unique dental needs. Comfort, safety, and personalization are prioritized across all procedures — from routine cleanings to complex work.

## What to Expect
1. **Thorough consultation** — dental history, concerns, and goals reviewed
2. **Comprehensive oral exam** — teeth, gums, bones, bite, and smile assessed; X-rays as needed
3. **Personalized treatment plan** — findings discussed; questions answered before any work begins
4. **Ongoing partnership** — each visit is designed to be informative, comfortable, and positive
