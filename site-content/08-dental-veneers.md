## What Are Dental Veneers?
Thin shells adhered to the front surface of teeth. Dramatically improve color, shape, size, and length. More stain-resistant than natural tooth enamel.

## Benefits
- **Natural Appearance** — closely resemble natural teeth
- **Stain Resistance** — withstand coffee, wine, and other staining substances
- **Versatility** — correct chips, gaps, severe discoloration, misshapen teeth
- **Durability** — last up to 15 years with proper care
- **Minimal Maintenance** — regular brushing, flossing, and check-ups

## Veneers vs. Crowns
| | Veneers | Crowns |
|---|---|---|
| **Purpose** | Cosmetic improvements | Cosmetic & restorative |
| **Coverage** | Front surface only | Entire tooth |
| **Best for** | Minor imperfections (stains, chips, gaps) | Significant damage or decay |
| **Materials** | Porcelain or composite | Porcelain, ceramic, or metal alloys |
