# Clear Aligners
**URL:** https://bilskidental.com/cosmetic-dentistry/clear-aligners
**Tagline:** Transparent – Custom-Made – Seamless

## Overview
Nearly invisible, custom-made plastic shells that fit snugly over teeth and apply gentle pressure to reposition them. Ideal for mild to moderate orthodontic needs. Each aligner worn ~2 weeks, minimum 22 hours/day.

**Night-Time Wear Program also available:** worn after dinner through sleeping hours, one week at a time — shortens overall treatment duration.

## Conditions Treated
- Gapped teeth
- Cramped / overcrowded teeth
- Overbite / underbite
- Open bite
- Crooked teeth
- Crossbite
