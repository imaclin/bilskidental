## Overview
Dental bridges and partial dentures fill gaps from missing teeth. Prevents adjacent teeth from shifting, maintains natural jaw/bite alignment. Unaddressed gaps cause bite misalignment and difficulty chewing or speaking.

## Key Benefits of Dental Bridges
- **Aesthetic Restoration** — natural, appealing smile
- **Enhanced Functionality** — unimpeded speech and comfortable eating
- **Facial Structure Maintenance** — prevents sagging of facial muscles
- **Prevention of Tooth Migration** — stops surrounding teeth from shifting

## Bridge Procedure (3 Phases)
1. **Preparation Phase** — reshape abutment teeth; take impressions; place temporary bridge
2. **Health Evaluation** — assess gum health; trial bridge framework fit
3. **Bridge Finalization** — precise adjustments; cement permanent bridge; care guidance provided

## Removable Partial Dentures
Versatile, cost-effective solution for partial tooth loss. Custom-designed for comfort and aesthetics.

### Partial Denture Procedure
1. **Initial Consultation** — detailed impressions of dental arches taken
2. **Fabrication** — models sent to dental lab for custom partial denture
3. **Fitting Session** — return after a few weeks; adjustments made for perfect fit
4. **Longevity** — expected lifespan 5–7 years; periodic adjustments for optimal comfort
