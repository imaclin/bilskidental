# Advanced AI Dentistry
**URL:** https://bilskidental.com/advanced-ai-dentistry

## Overview
Bilski Dental integrates Artificial Intelligence into diagnostic and treatment processes for greater efficiency and precision. AI modernizes patient care across diagnostics, treatment planning, and procedure execution.

## AI in Dental Diagnostics
- **Enhanced X-ray Analysis** — AI algorithms analyze density variations in teeth to detect cavities, fractures, and anomalies
- **Visual Markings** — AI marks problem areas on X-rays in distinct colors, making issues easy to understand for both dentist and patient
- **Integration with imaging systems** — enhances clarity and interpretation of dental X-rays

## AI in Treatment Planning & Execution

### Implant Dentistry
- AI analyzes jawbone structure and density to plan the **exact location** for implant placement
- Ensures optimal placement and minimizes surgical risks

### Invisalign & Clear Aligners
- AI helps design the **precise placement of aligner buttons** that facilitate effective tooth movement
- Ensures each aligner fits perfectly and works efficiently

## The Bilski Dental AI Advantage
- Improves treatment accuracy
- Enhances patient understanding and involvement in their care
- Allows forecasting of treatment outcomes — patients can see potential results **before treatment begins**
