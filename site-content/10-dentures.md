## Overview
Both traditional and digital dentures available. Focused on restoring smile beauty, functionality, and self-confidence. More natural-looking and comfortable than ever.

## Types of Dentures

### Full Dentures
- Ideal for complete tooth replacement
- **Conventional:** placed after healing period
- **Immediate:** placed directly post-extraction
- Crafted for natural appearance and comfort

### Partial Dentures
- Perfect when some natural teeth remain
- Options: **removable** or **fixed (bridges)**
- Designed to blend seamlessly with existing teeth

## Custom Dental Solutions
Bilski Dental custom denture services restore full function and appearance of the smile. Alternatives like implants also available and recommended where appropriate.
