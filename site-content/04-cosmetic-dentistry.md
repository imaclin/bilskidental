# Cosmetic Dentistry
**URL:** https://bilskidental.com/cosmetic-dentistry
**Tagline:** Functional – Seamless – Natural-Looking

## Overview
Cosmetic dentistry improves the visual appeal of your smile while contributing to overall well-being. Confidence in your appearance encourages better oral hygiene and regular dental care, leading to improved long-term oral health.

## Services

### Clear Aligners
Crooked or crowded teeth corrected with nearly invisible aligners. Brands: **Invisalign, 3Shape, Clear Correct**. Custom-made, comfortable; ideal for mild–moderate orthodontic needs.
→ [Learn More](/cosmetic-dentistry/clear-aligners)

### Veneers
Three options: **Same-Day, Porcelain, and Snap-On**. Enhances color, shape, size, and length. Durable and stain resistant.
→ [Learn More](/cosmetic-dentistry/veneers)

### Teeth Whitening
In-office and customized at-home treatments. Professional oversight ensures safety and effectiveness.
→ [Learn More](/cosmetic-dentistry/teeth-whitening)

### Gum Lift (Gingivectomy)
Sculpts gum tissue to correct uneven gum lines. Reduces excessive gum visibility. Improves smile aesthetics and gum health by reducing bacteria-prone areas.
→ [Learn More](/cosmetic-dentistry/gum-lift)

### Crowns
Tooth-shaped caps restoring size, shape, strength, and appearance. Materials: porcelain, ceramic, or metal alloys matched to natural teeth.
→ [Learn More](/cosmetic-dentistry/crowns)

### Dentures
Full or partial dentures restore beauty, functionality, and self-confidence after tooth loss.
→ [Learn More](/cosmetic-dentistry/dentures)

### Cosmetic Implants
Permanent, natural-looking prosthetics for missing teeth. Services include: smile restoration, advanced bone grafting, full mouth reconstruction, bone and gum regeneration.
→ [Learn More](/cosmetic-dentistry/cosmetic-implants)
