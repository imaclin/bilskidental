## Overview
General dentistry preserves oral health through preventive care and early detection of dental problems. Routine check-ups and cleanings catch cavities and gum disease early. Patient education on effective oral hygiene is a key focus.

## Services

### Dentures
Traditional and effective solution for missing teeth. Restores smile, speech, and eating function. Boosts self-confidence and well-being.
→ [Learn More](/cosmetic-dentistry/dentures)

### Bridges & Partials
Fills gaps from missing teeth to prevent adjacent teeth from shifting. Maintains jaw alignment and bite function. Prevents bite misalignment and chewing/speaking difficulty.
→ [Learn More](/general-dentistry/bridges-and-partials)

### Family Dentistry
Comprehensive dental care for all ages — children through adults. Covers routine cleanings, exams, and oral health education tailored to each life stage.
→ [Learn More](/family-dentistry-routine-exams)

### Laser Gum Therapy
High-intensity laser periodontal therapy — first of its kind in the region. Removes inflamed gum tissue around tooth roots. Eliminates bacteria and promotes healthy gum regeneration. Precision targeting preserves healthy tissue and speeds healing.
→ [Learn More](/laser-gum-therapy)

### Root Canals
Treats infections in the tooth pulp (caused by decay, cracks, or chips). Removes infected pulp, cleans canal system, seals tooth. Relieves pain and prevents further damage.
→ [Learn More](/general-dentistry/root-canal)

### Wisdom Teeth
Third molars typically appear ages 17–25. Extraction recommended for impaction, crowding, or risk of damage to adjacent teeth.
→ [Learn More](/general-dentistry/wisdom-teeth)

### General Implants
Artificial roots anchored in jawbone. Supports crowns, bridges, or dentures. Mimics natural tooth strength and appearance. Maintains face shape.
→ [Learn More](/general-dentistry/general-implants)
